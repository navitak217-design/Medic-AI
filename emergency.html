Heart Attack

Overview

A heart attack happens when blood flow to the heart is blocked.

Symptoms

  • Chest pain or pressure
  • Pain in arm, neck, jaw
  • Shortness of breath
  • Sweating and nausea

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Keep patient calm and seated
  • Don’t: Do not delay emergency help

First Aid

  • Call emergency services immediately
  • Loosen tight clothes
  • Give aspirin only if prescribed

Stroke

Overview

Stroke occurs when blood supply to the brain stops.

Symptoms

  • Face drooping
  • Weak arm or leg
  • Slurred speech

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Call emergency immediately
  • Don’t: Do not give food or water

First Aid

  • Lay patient on side
  • Keep head slightly raised

High BP (Hypertensive Crisis)

Overview

Very high blood pressure can damage organs.

Symptoms

  • Severe headache
  • Nose bleeding
  • Chest pain

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Keep patient relaxed
  • Don’t: Avoid physical activity

First Aid

  • Seek emergency medical care
  • Do not give unprescribed medicine

Low BP (Shock)

Overview

Low BP reduces blood flow to vital organs.

Symptoms

  • Dizziness
  • Cold skin
  • Fainting

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Raise legs slightly
  • Don’t: Do not make patient stand

First Aid

  • Lay patient flat
  • Cover with blanket

Asthma Attack

Overview

Asthma attack causes difficulty in breathing.

Symptoms

  • Wheezing sound
  • Chest tightness
  • Shortness of breath

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Help use inhaler
  • Don’t: Do not panic

First Aid

  • Seat patient upright
  • Repeat inhaler if needed

Severe Bleeding

Overview

Heavy bleeding can be life-threatening.

Symptoms

  • Continuous bleeding
  • Weakness

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Apply pressure
  • Don’t: Remove objects from wound

First Aid

  • Use clean cloth
  • Apply firm pressure

Burns

Overview

Burns skin damage hoti hai jo heat, chemicals, electricity ya fire se hoti hai.

Symptoms

  • Red or blistered skin
  • Severe pain
  • Swelling

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Burn area ko thanday pani se thanda karo
  • Don’t: Ice, oil, toothpaste ya cream na lagao

First Aid

  • 10–15 minutes tak running water lagao
  • Clean cloth se cover karo
  • Severe burn me hospital le jao

Snake Bite

Overview

Snake bite me venom body me enter ho sakta hai jo dangerous hota hai.

Symptoms

  • Swelling and pain at bite area
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Breathing difficulty

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Patient ko bilkul still rakho
  • Don’t: Bite ko cut ya suck na karo

First Aid

  • Bite area ko immobilize karo
  • Tight items remove karo
  • Immediately hospital le jao

Food Poisoning

Overview

Contaminated food ya water se food poisoning hoti hai.

Symptoms

  • Vomiting
  • Diarrhea
  • Stomach cramps

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Fluids aur ORS do
  • Don’t: Junk aur spicy food na do

First Aid

  • ORS solution do
  • Rest karne do
  • Severe case me doctor se consult

Acute Respiratory Failure

Overview

Is condition me lungs body ko sufficient oxygen nahi de pate.

Symptoms

  • Severe breathing problem
  • Blue lips or fingers
  • Confusion or anxiety

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Emergency services call karo
  • Don’t: Patient ko akela na choro

First Aid

  • Patient ko upright position me bithao
  • Airway clear rakho
  • Immediate medical help lo
