Fever

Overview

Fever is a temporary increase in body temperature, often caused by infection.

Symptoms

  • High body temperature
  • Chills and sweating
  • Body pain and weakness

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Drink plenty of fluids
  • Don’t: Overdress the patient

First Aid

  • Lukewarm sponging
  • Paracetamol if advised by a doctor

Diabetes

Overview

Diabetes is a condition in which blood sugar levels remain high.

Symptoms

  • Frequent urination
  • Increased thirst
  • Fatigue

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Follow a balanced diet
  • Don’t: Eat excessive sugary foods

First Aid

  • Check blood sugar levels
  • Give sugary drink if blood sugar is low

Dengue

Overview

Dengue is a viral infection transmitted by mosquitoes.

Symptoms

  • High fever
  • Severe headache
  • Joint and muscle pain

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Drink fluids and rest
  • Don’t: Take aspirin or ibuprofen

First Aid

  • Monitor platelet count
  • Consult a doctor immediately

Malaria

Overview

Malaria is caused by parasites transmitted through mosquito bites.

Symptoms

  • High fever with chills
  • Sweating
  • Weakness

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Use mosquito protection
  • Don’t: Delay medical treatment

First Aid

  • Get blood tests done
  • Take prescribed antimalarial medicines

COVID-19

Overview

COVID-19 is a contagious viral disease affecting the respiratory system.

Symptoms

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Loss of taste or smell

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Wear a mask and isolate
  • Don’t: Visit crowded places

First Aid

  • Rest and stay hydrated
  • Monitor oxygen levels

Typhoid

Overview

Typhoid is a bacterial infection spread through contaminated food and water.

Symptoms

  • Persistent fever
  • Abdominal pain
  • Weakness

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Drink clean water
  • Don’t: Eat unhygienic food

First Aid

  • Consult a doctor
  • Complete antibiotic course

Anemia

Overview

Anemia occurs when the body lacks enough healthy red blood cells.

Symptoms

  • Fatigue
  • Pale skin
  • Dizziness

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Eat iron-rich foods
  • Don’t: Skip meals

First Aid

  • Iron supplements (if prescribed)
  • Blood test for confirmation

Dehydration

Overview

Dehydration happens when the body loses more fluids than it takes in.

Symptoms

  • Dry mouth
  • Dark urine
  • Dizziness

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Drink water and ORS
  • Don’t: Drink alcohol

First Aid

  • Give ORS solution
  • Rest in a cool place

Migraine

Overview

Migraine is a neurological condition causing severe headaches.

Symptoms

  • Throbbing headache
  • Nausea
  • Sensitivity to light

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Rest in a dark room
  • Don’t: Expose to loud noise

First Aid

  • Pain relief medication
  • Cold compress on head

Gastritis

Overview

Gastritis is inflammation of the stomach lining.

Symptoms

  • Stomach pain
  • Nausea
  • Bloating

Do’s & Don’ts

  • Do: Eat light meals
  • Don’t: Eat spicy or oily food

First Aid

  • Use antacids
  • Consult a doctor if pain continues
